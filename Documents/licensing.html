Land and Coastline Data

Data relating to land, including coastline boundaries, is sourced from OpenStreetMap (OSM).

OSM data is made available under the Open Database Licence version 1.0 (the ODbL).

Note also that information about the Caspian Sea is also sourced from OpenStreetMap.

Marine Data

The names and extents of oceans, seas and other open bodies of water are sourced from the International Hydrological Organisation.

Flanders Marine Institute (2021). Global Oceans and Seas, version 1. Available online at https://www.marineregions.org/. https://doi.org/10.14284/542.
